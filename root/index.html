I would reccomend that anyone who plays or wants to play Terraria to use projectile weapons based on this data.
Aswell as this from the data it is very clear that multi target weapons do more damage per second than single target weapons.
From both of these conclusions if you wanted to maximise damage per second in Terraria, using a projectile weapon that does damage to multiple targets is highly advisable.
